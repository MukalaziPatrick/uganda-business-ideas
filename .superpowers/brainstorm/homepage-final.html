DESIGN NOTES
Colors: Deep forest green #1C3A2A + warm gold #F5C842 (Uganda Sunrise)
Headlines: Georgia serif for hero + section titles. Segoe UI for body.
Hero: Centred, dual CTA — "Browse Ideas" (gold) + "Find Work" (outline)
Pillars: Post a Job / Find Work — 2-column, sits directly under hero
Ideas: Magazine grid — 1 featured (full-width, dark green) + 2 smaller cards
Jobs teaser: 3 horizontal rows with salary visible. "See all jobs" link.
Nav (desktop): Logo left, links centre, "Post a Job" CTA button right
Nav (mobile): Logo left, hamburger right — drawer slides in
Footer: Dark green background, gold logo, all page links
Mobile: All sections stack vertically. Pillars become full-width. Ideas grid stays 2-col.
