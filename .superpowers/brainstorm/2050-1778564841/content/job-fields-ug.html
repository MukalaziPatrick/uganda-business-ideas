What details should a job listing show?

Uganda-specific — what matters most to a job seeker here. Select all that should be required when posting a job.

✓

Job Title + Skill Category

e.g. "Carpenter", "Boda rider", "Tailor", "Data entry clerk" — pick from a Uganda skills list

✓

District + Town/Stage

Not just "Kampala" but "Kampala → Bwaise" or "Wakiso → Nansana". Where exactly must the person report?

✓

Pay — daily / weekly / monthly

UGX amount. Show pay period clearly. Many jobs in UG pay daily (construction) or weekly (market work).

✓

Job Type — Permanent / Casual / Contract

Permanent (long-term), Casual (by the day), Contract (fixed weeks/months). Very important in Uganda context.

✓

Gender preference (optional)

Some Uganda employers specify. Show it transparently so applicants know upfront and aren't wasting time.

✓

Minimum Education

None required / PLE / UCE (O-level) / UACE (A-level) / Certificate / Diploma / Degree. Common filter in UG.

✓

Accommodation / Food provided?

Very relevant for domestic workers, farm workers, guards. Yes / No / Negotiable.

✓

Contact method — WhatsApp / Call / Walk-in

Many Uganda employers prefer walk-ins or calls over online forms. Let employer choose how to receive applicants.
