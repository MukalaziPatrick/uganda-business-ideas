How should the two apps connect?

LocateUG (uganda-map) is already fully built — map, pins, search, claims, verification. Uganda Business Ideas is your ideas + WhatsApp traffic app. Here are 3 ways to bring them together.

A

Embed LocateUG inside the Business Ideas app

Use the existing /embed/[tag] widget from LocateUG. Embed it as an iframe section on the Business Ideas homepage. Add a /jobs page to Business Ideas. Two separate apps, one page links them.

Pros

  • Zero new backend work
  • LocateUG stays standalone
  • Ships in days

Cons

  • Iframe feels disconnected
  • No shared design language
  • Jobs lives in a different codebase
B

Redesign Business Ideas as the hub, deep-link to LocateUG (Recommended)

Rebuild the Business Ideas homepage with the 3-pillar hero (Ideas | Businesses | Jobs). "Find Businesses" links to LocateUG at its own URL. Add /jobs page inside Business Ideas. Same visual design on both apps.

Pros

  • Both apps keep their own domain
  • Shared design = feels like one platform
  • Jobs built once in Business Ideas
  • LocateUG gets traffic from hub

Cons

  • User navigates between 2 domains
  • Slightly more design work upfront
C

Merge everything into LocateUG as the main platform

LocateUG becomes the master app. Business ideas, jobs, and the map all live under one roof. Business Ideas app redirects to LocateUG. One URL, one codebase.

Pros

  • One app, one URL, one brand
  • Everything integrated natively
  • Long-term cleanest architecture

Cons

  • Most work — migrate all content
  • Lose ugandabusinessideas.com SEO
  • Weeks not days to ship
