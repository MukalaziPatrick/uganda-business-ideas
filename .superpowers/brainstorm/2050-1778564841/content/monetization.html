How do you make money from this?

You said people are visiting from WhatsApp but not buying. Which monetization fits your goals?

A

Featured / Promoted Listings

Businesses pay to appear at the top of search results or get a "Featured" badge. Free to list, pay to stand out.

  • Free basic listing for all businesses
  • Pay ~UGX 50k–200k/month to be featured
  • Featured = top of results + gold badge
  • Easy to sell via WhatsApp
B

Job Posting Fees

Free to browse jobs. Employers pay a small fee to post a vacancy. Workers browse and apply for free.

  • Post a job: ~UGX 20k–50k per post
  • Browse jobs: always free
  • Worker profiles: free
  • Revenue scales with job volume
C

Both — Featured Listings + Job Posts

Two revenue streams from day one. Businesses pay to be featured. Employers pay to post jobs. Everything else is free.

  • Featured business listings (monthly)
  • Paid job postings
  • Browsing always free for users
  • Most revenue potential early on
