How do businesses get listed?

You mentioned scraping businesses registered online + people submitting their own. Which model do you want to start with?

A

Self-Submit First

Business owners fill a form to add their business. You review and approve. Slower to grow but higher quality data, and owners are invested.

  • Simple "List My Business" form
  • You approve before it goes live
  • Owner can edit their profile later
  • WhatsApp CTA to get submissions
B

You Seed It, They Claim It

You manually add businesses you find online. Owners can then "claim" their listing and update it. Starts with real data immediately.

  • Admin panel to add businesses
  • "Is this your business? Claim it" button
  • Claiming via WhatsApp or email
  • Grows as you add more data
C

Both at Once

Open submissions from day 1, AND you seed with data you find. Two pipelines filling the same directory.

  • Public "List My Business" form
  • Admin panel for bulk entry
  • All listings go through approval
  • Fastest way to build volume
