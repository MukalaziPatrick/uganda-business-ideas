How should the Jobs section work?

You want people to find jobs near them by skill. How do job seekers and employers connect?

A

Simple Job Board

Businesses post job openings. Job seekers browse by skill + location. Apply via WhatsApp or phone call. No accounts needed to browse.

  • Job listings: title, skill, location, pay, contact
  • Filter by skill (carpenter, driver, nurse…)
  • Filter by district
  • "Apply on WhatsApp" button
B

Worker Profiles + Job Board

Job seekers create a skill profile. Employers can also browse workers, not just post jobs. Two-sided marketplace.

  • Everything in A, plus…
  • Worker registers: skill, district, experience
  • Employer browses worker profiles
  • Contact worker via WhatsApp
C

Start Simple, Add Profiles Later

Launch with the job board only (A). Add worker profiles in phase 2 once you have traffic. Lower risk, faster launch.

  • Phase 1: job board only
  • Phase 2: worker profiles after launch
  • Proven demand before building more
  • Recommended for faster shipping
