What should this platform become?

You want to grow beyond "business ideas" into a full Uganda business & jobs platform. Which vision fits best?

A

Business Discovery Hub

Show real businesses on a map. People find shops, services, farms near them. Business owners can list themselves. You grow a local directory Uganda doesn't have yet.

  • Business map with location pins
  • Business profiles (name, contact, category, hours)
  • Search by area / category
  • Jobs section attached to each business
B

Jobs + Business Combined

Two equal pillars: find businesses near you AND find jobs near you by skill. Businesses post jobs. Workers browse by skill match and location.

  • Business directory with map
  • Jobs board with skill-based filtering
  • Businesses can post vacancies
  • Workers create skill profiles
C

Full Uganda Economic Platform

Business ideas + business directory + jobs + future recruitment. Everything in one place. Biggest vision, most ambitious to build.

  • All of A + B above
  • Business ideas (existing)
  • Verified / registered business listings
  • Recruitment tools (future phase)
